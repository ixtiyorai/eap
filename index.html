in due course

May 21, 2023
/ɪn djuː kɔːrs/

Meaning

English: At the appropriate time; eventually; when the time is right.

Uzbek: o'zining vaqti kelganda, to'g'ri vaqtda

Example

English: If you still can't take an IELTS exam, don't worry. You will definitely do it in due course.

Uzbek: Agar sen hali ham IELTS imtihonini topshira olmayotgan bo'lsang, qo'rqma. Vaqti kelib albatta topshirasan.

Usage Tips

This phrase is formal and is often used in professional or academic contexts. It implies patience and trust in the natural progression of events.

Synonyms: eventually, in time, at the appropriate time, when the time is right
